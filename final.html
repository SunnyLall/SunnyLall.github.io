Final Project

For my final project I made a game very similar to one called "Flappy Bird". The objective of the game
is to fly your character through a set of pipes. This is done by pressing the "space bar" repeatedly.
If you hit the pipes, then you will die and you will be brought to a "game over" screen.
In order to play again just click return home and then the "Start" button.
